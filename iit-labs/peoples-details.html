Vivek Pandey

PhD

MEMS based piezoresistive sensors, Photolithographic patterning of devices, Thin film solar cells, Graphene , Exploring Hot Wire Chemical Vapor Deposition (HWCVD) technique for different type of barrier layer coatings on thin film devices.

M.Sc. in Physics (specialization in electronics and radio Physics) from T M Bhagalpur University, Bihar. Joined PhD programme at IIT Bombay in 2010.

  • Vivek Pandey, Lavanya Sanagavarapu and Rajiv Dusane,” Fabrication of High Gauge Factor Piezoresistive nanocrystalline Si Films using Aluminum Induced Crystallization of HWCVD deposited a-Si:H”, Canadian Journal of Physics, 2014, 92(7/8): 749-752,
  • Vivek Pandey and Rajiv Dusane, “Low temperature aluminum induced crystallization of HWCVD deposited a-Si:H”, IEEE explore doi 10.1109/ICANMEET.2013.6609307
  • Vivek Pandey, M P Gururajan and Rajiv Dusane “Aluminum induced crystallization of a-Si:H in Si/Al/Si layered structure”, ICEMP 2014 proceedings
